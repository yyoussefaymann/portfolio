Bootcamp 2020: Slideshow project (Juice WRLD)

My first attempt at a programming project after the two week bootcamp 2020. my project displays broad understanding of images, sounds, arrays, and conditionals. I designed and created a slideshow about my favourite rapper where it contains exactly 5 slides each with a 900x500 picture of him in addition to simple text in each slide. You could flip through the slides either by clicking the mouse, pressing the right arrow, or just waiting and the slide will flip automatically. on the other hand if you want to go to the previous slide just press the left arrow. in addition, using the tint() function the slides fade into each other in a smooth manner. Finally while watching the slideshow and reading the simple information written, Juice WRLD's most famous song 'Lucid Dreams' will be playing.

Check out the code on Github: https://github.com/yyoussefaymann/adbd977.git
